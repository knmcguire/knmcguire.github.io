## Ever since I was litle 

For as long as I can remember. Perhaps my older sister made me play on her gameboy sometimes when I was just a few years old, and that for sure got me hooked! Who knows but one of my most vivid memories of my childhood was playing Donkey Kong on my Super Nintendo which we got at home when I was perhaps 6 years old. 

Gaming has been my most cnsoistent hobby, as most other sports and hobbys went and gone (although I have played tennis for 25 years but hardly pick up a racket now an days), gaming stayed and remaind. I still mourn the loss of my SNES, Playstation 1, Gamebody advance that my parents sold at the flea marketat Dutch Koniningen dag, so now I hold on to my consoles! Currently I am the proud owner of a Nitendo Switch, Wii, Orignal Xbox, Playstation 3,4,5 and a meta quest 2.. This goes along with a great selection of the best (in my opinion) games per platform there is so far.
